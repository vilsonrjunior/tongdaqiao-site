ABOUT US

Building trusted bridges between businesses, cultures and opportunities.

Tongda Qiao Group Ltd is an international sourcing and trading company committed to connecting businesses with trusted suppliers across global markets.

Inspired by the meaning of our name "Tong Da Qiao" (通达桥), or "Bridge to Connectivity and Success" we believe successful international trade is built on trust, transparency and long-term partnerships.

From supplier identification and procurement to logistics coordination and quality assurance, we help our clients navigate international trade with confidence.
